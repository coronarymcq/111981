About Us

The website

This website is a personal space I built to showcase and organize all of my work and contributions throughout medical school. It's designed not just as a resource hub, but as a reflection of the effort and dedication I’ve put into collecting, curating, and developing valuable academic content. As a student at the Faculty of Medicine at BAU, I wanted a platform where I could centralize everything — from MCQ banks to study our batch materials — in one accessible, user-friendly location. Currently, our batch Exam Bank covering various subjects and systems are available for review. Click here to explore resources from other batches, or other stuff if available.

The Team

Coronary MCQS

It started as a small initiative in the summer semester of 2023, created by a first-year medical student on Telegram. What began as a simple platform for sharing and solving questions among classmates quickly developed into a valuable resource for hundreds of students. This initiative has grown thanks to the active participation of many others, who contribute by writing questions and ensuring our content stays diverse and relevant.
