About Us

The website

I created this website to bring all resources together in one place, making it easier for students, specifically for students of the Faculty of Medicine at BAU, like me, to access valuable content. As a student, I built this website with the goal of providing a clear and user-friendly experience. The idea is to create a safe, all-in one space where users can access the Q banks we have collected over the years, including our batch and so the previous ones. Currently, our batch MCQs banks of different subjects and systems are now available and ready for review. Click here to check for other batches, if available.

As a medical student and new to web development field, I’m committed to ensure the website is updated on an scheduled basis, so others can access the most up-to-date Q banks. Soon later, we'll also consider posting USMLE® questions with the specified content of each system.

The Team

Coronary MCQS

It started as a small initiative in the summer semester of 2023, created by a first-year medical student on Telegram. What began as a simple platform for sharing and solving questions among classmates quickly developed into a valuable resource for hundreds of students. This initiative has grown thanks to the active participation of many others, who contribute by writing questions and ensuring our content stays diverse and relevant.

The Present

As we continue to expand, Coronary MCQS remains committed to delivering free, high-level, reliable multiple-choice questions tailored specifically for medical students. Our focus is shifting towards creating even more specialized content, especially for the clinical years, where the need for practical, scenario-based learning is crucial. With contributions from students, we ensure that our questions reflect real-world medical cases, helping students stay prepared for the challenges ahead. We're excited to keep growing and providing valuable resources for years to come.

    • The logo

    The logo is simple yet incorporates several important elements that I aimed to highlight within its compact design. The heart represents our batch name, the 2028 Coronary Batch. The merged question mark within the heart symbolize our main goal: to provide questions in the form of MCQs, as also indicated by the text inside. The heartbeat line signifies life, representing our commitment to persevere and support other students. The light blue to gray (or white if the theme is dark) color scheme in a logo signifies health and academic style while also conveying trust and consistency.
